TS – 3040 Vision Tool Pre-Setter

TS-3040 Tool Pre-Setter is the latest series of Vision Tool Pre-Setters. The measurement is automatic, Error free and accurate. The Machine Base is made out of Close Grained Cast Iron, thermally stabilised. The Column is of heat-treated Aluminium alloy. The axis movement is on Super Precision Lenear Ball Guides, grease Lubricated for life. Each axis contains Two Rails and Four Blocks for stable and error free movement. Axis movement is manual and pneumatically declamped by single button and fine movement is by Hand Knob. Axis measurement is by imported precision incremental coded optical lenear Scale and encoder. Spindle is mounted on super precision Spindle Bearings for the high accuracy and is grease lubricated for life. Tool Holder clamping is either Vacuum or Power clamping by Pneumatic Cylinder. Spindle Braking is provided for holding the spindle is position during adjustment of tool. Tool Image is displayed on the 12.1” Touch Screen Display. Image Processing System is imported from Germany. 1000 memory of Tool Data Storage is available in the system. The Tool Image can be anywhere on the screen and the dynamic cross hair is will take the position of the image and will give the measurement. Various Quick Adaptors such as ISO / BT/ HSK / VDI / CAPTO is offered. Front illumination for Inspection of Tool Tip, Thermal Label Printer for Printing Tool measurement is also offered as optional. Test Mandrel and the Machine cabinet are offered as a standard. Data Transfer facility is provided with RS232 and USB ports.

TS – A7 Tool Pre-Setter

Started manufacturing TS – A7 Tool Pre-Setter from 1990 under collaboration from M/s.Kyoritsu Seiki of Japan. More than 500Nos Tool Pre-Setter has been sold since 1990. The Machine Base is made out of close Grained Cast Iron thermally stabilised and machined. The Column is manufactured from Alloy Steel casehardened and ground. The Axis movement and rotation of spindle is by Hand Wheel. The measurement is by Precision Optical Lenear Scales and the readings are displayed on the counter. Tool clamping is by Pneumatic Cylinder which ensures proper seating of Adaptor Taper in to the spindle taper ensuring high accuracy. The optical Projector with 20X magnification is offered and is imported from Germany. Dial Indicator for Horizontal and Vertical is provided. Various Quick Adaptor such as ISO / BT/ HSK / VDI / CAPTO can be provided. Test Mandrel and the machine cabinet are offered as standard.
